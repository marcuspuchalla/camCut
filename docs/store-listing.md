## Name

Baby Cam Cut

## Subtitle / short description (≤ 80 chars)

Look and listen from another device — picture and sound, peer-to-peer.

## Description

Turn one of your devices into a camera and watch the live picture — with
sound — on another. Point a spare phone or a laptop webcam at the crib,
optionally crop to just the spot you care about, and open the link on your
phone. The video and audio travel directly between your two devices,
peer-to-peer; there is no account, no cloud, no recording, and no ads.

- Any device with a browser and a camera can be the camera
- Crop to a region, or stream the full frame
- Sound comes through untouched — no "noise cleanup" that erases quiet sounds
- A live clock on the picture shows the stream is still coming through
- One permanent link — share it with your partner once
- Offline hotel mode: link two phones over a hotspot with QR codes, no internet
- Free, open source (GPL-3.0), non-commercial — built by a parent, for parents

**Please read — not a safety device.** Baby Cam Cut only shows you what the
camera sees and hears. It does not watch your child for you — not
automatically, not continuously. It detects nothing (no breathing, no
movement, no crying, no danger) and it is not a medical device. The stream
can stop at any time without warning, and the failure may not be shown to
you. Use it at your own risk, for healthy children, as a convenience — it
never replaces having an adult nearby.
